# Business Performance Summary
A medium-sized company named SmartTech Electronics wants to monitor its financial health.
The CFO has requested a Power BI dashboard that visualizes revenues, expenses, and 
profitability trends, helping management make data-driven budgeting and investment decisions.

## Overview
- **Total Sales:** $118M  
- **Total Profit:** $16M  
- **Profit Margin:** 14% → Moderate-level performance according to general business benchmarks  

## Profit Drivers
- **Highest Profit Product:** Paseo  
- **Lowest Profit Product:** Carretera  
- **Highest Profit Country:** France  
- **Lowest Profit Country:** Mexico  
- **Highest Profit Customer Segment:** Government  
- **Lowest Profit Customer Segment:** Enterprise  

## Growth Analysis
- **Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth:** 249%  
  > Note: This percentage is inflated because the dataset covers only 16 months, not a full 2 years  
- **Adjusted Growth Measure** (same 4 months comparison over 2 years): 136%  

## Cost & Pricing Insights
- **Optimal Manufacturing Cost:** $10 per product  
- **Impact of Manufacturing Cost:**  
  - Too low → decreases product quality → lowers profit  
  - Too high → increases cost → lowers profit  

## Profit Trends
- **Highest Monthly Profit:** $3.5M in November (over 2 years)  
- **Discount Strategy Impact:**  
  - Highest profits occur with **Low Band Discounts**, followed by Medium Band  
  - Increasing or removing discounts completely has an **inverse effect on profit**  

## Forecast
- If current trends continue, **expected profit for 2015:** $22M
